Pizza Recipe

Description

This is my favourite dish and it is pretty easy to make!

american pizza < italian pizza

Ingredients:

  • 70g Mozzarella
  • 5g basil
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil

For the dough:

  • 105 ml water
  • 175g flour
  • 0,6g dry yeats
  • 3,6g salt
  • 5g olive oil
  • 2g sugar

For the sauce:

  • 100g tomatoes
  • 1/4 garlic clove
  • 1g salt
  • 0,8g oregano

Steps:

  1. Mix all ingredients for the dough and let it rest for 20 minutes
  2. Mix all ingredients for the sauce and put it to the side
  3. Pre heat oven to 200 Degrees
  4. Form the dough and use the sauce and Mozzarella on the dough
  5. Cook for 15 minutes
