Pasta Recipe

Description

Pasta is very nice if made with the perfect timer for the noodles to cook!

Ingredients:

  • 500g noodles (of your choice)
  • 500g canned tomatoes
  • 2 TS butter
  • 1 TS flour
  • 1 cup of cream

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles in boiling water for 7 minutes
  2. Melt the butter, add the flour and stir it up well
  3. Add the cream
  4. Add the tomatoes
  5. Let it cook for 10 minutes
